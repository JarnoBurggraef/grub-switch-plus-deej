4_misc
======
Various community contributions useful for making GRUBswitches:


3Dprint_Toggleswitch_Stand_STL
------------------------------
3d-printable stand for mounting recommended GRUBswitch toggle switches
by Ruediger Willenberg


ArduinoMicro_Toggleswitch_Fritzing
----------------------------------
Fritzing diagram for connecting a toggle switch to Arduino Micro
by Pina Merkert


Lasercut_Case_ProMicro_Toggleswitch_SVG
---------------------------------------
Lasercut files for mountable case that fits SparkFun Pro Micro and a toggle switch
by Pina Merkert and Klaus Greff
